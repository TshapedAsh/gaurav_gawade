Master's Thesis

This master's thesis represents the culmination of my graduate research in astrophysics. It provides an in-depth analysis and discussion of my research work, including theoretical frameworks, observational methodologies, and data-driven insights into Dual Active Galactic Nuclei in high-energy window, mostly focused on X-ray regime. Through rigorous exploration and innovative techniques, the thesis aims to advance our understanding of key astrophysical phenomena.
