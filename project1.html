ASI Poster Presentation

This presentation, titled "Investigating Dual AGN/SMBH: A Study of Dual AGN MRK739 in High Energy Astrophysics", was delivered at the ASI event. In this work, I explore the dynamics of dual active galactic nuclei (AGN) in merging galaxies. By analyzing multi-wavelength observations from Swift, NuSTAR, XMM-Newton, and Chandra, the study estimates a projected nuclear separation of approximately 3.6 kpc in Mrk 739. The poster details the methodology behind data acquisition, image analysis, and comparative studies with similar systems like NGC 6240 to shed light on galaxy evolution and supermassive black hole interactions.
